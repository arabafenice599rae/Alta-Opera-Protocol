Liquidity by Math,
Not by Promises.

AltaOpera is a quadratic bonding curve ERC20 with on-chain exit liquidity, deterministic pricing, and transparent protocol fees.

The contract itself acts as the autonomous market maker between the token and ETH. There are no external LPs to withdraw and no DEX pool that can be drained overnight. Buy and sell prices are fully determined by a mathematical curve.

The protocol is fully transparent and auditable. To explore the smart contracts, understand the mathematical model, or review the security architecture, please refer to the resources below.
